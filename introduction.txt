pack’n’ship (app)-
Our pack’n’ship app provides on the go packing and shipping services for the customers which is reliable,
fast and easy to use. Just open the app, choose your desired service and you are ready to go. 
We also provide our own transportation in case you may need it.
On our careers option within the app, people can join our team and help customers with their stuff. 
Joined members can choose their desired timing to work within the app and manage all sorts of their things including their paychecks, etc
